### What Scout Does
1. **Searches** for mechanical engineer roles (60%+ fit minimum)
2. **Posts** opportunities to Telegram with summaries
3. **Waits** for your "research" signal
4. **Researches** company (who, what, culture, stability)
5. **Creates** detailed application brief (sections 1-3)
6. **Builds** tailored resume + cover letter from your templates
7. **Tracks** all applications and outcomes

### What You Do
1. **Respond** to Scout's job posts ("research" / "skip")
2. **Review** company research briefly
3. **Approve** before Scout builds materials ("build materials" / "pass")
4. **Review** resume and cover letter
5. **Submit** applications (Scout helps you prepare)
6. **Track** outcomes (Scout logs results)

## 💡 Tips for Success

1. **Respond fast** — When Scout finds a good fit, say "research" same day
2. **Review Scout's work** — Application briefs are well-researched; read them before approving "build materials"
3. **Give feedback** — Tell Scout what you think about roles/companies (helps refine future searches)
4. **Submit promptly** — Once materials are ready, apply within 1-2 days (job market moves fast)
5. **Follow up** — Scout will remind you, but stay on top of applications
6. **Track outcomes** — Tell Scout results (interviews, rejections, etc.) so everyone learns
