# Job Analysis Quick Reference

## How to Get Scout to Analyze a Job

### Scenario 1: You Find a Job on Seek/LinkedIn

**What to do:**
1. Copy job title and company name
2. Send to Scout in Telegram

**Message format:**
```
Job Title: Production Engineer
Company: [Company Name]
Location: [City, State]
```

**Scout will:**
- Search for the full job posting
- Analyze your fit
- Give you a score and recommendation

---

### Scenario 2: You Want to Paste the Entire Job Description

**What to do:**
1. Open the job on Seek/LinkedIn/Indeed
2. Copy the ENTIRE job posting
3. Paste it here

**Message format:**
```
Analyze this job:

[paste entire job posting]
```

**Scout will:**
- Extract title, company, requirements
- Assess your fit immediately
- Ask if you want full materials

---

### Scenario 3: You Have Partial Details

**What to do:**
- Share whatever details you have

**Message format:**
```
Job Title: Design Engineer
Company: Telwater
Location: Coomera, Gold Coast
Requirements: CAD, mechanical design, tooling
```

**Scout will:**
- Fill in the gaps via research
- Analyze and score

## What Scout Analyzes

### Fit Score (0-100%)

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 80%+ | Excellent Fit | **APPLY NOW** |
| 70-79% | Very Good Fit | **Worth pursuing** |
| 60-69% | Good Fit | **Consider it** |
| <60% | Poor Fit | **Skip it** |

### Fit Assessment Covers

✅ Your education match (Engineering degree, Fabrication cert)
✅ Experience alignment (Habitec, Swift Marine, Superior Stainless)
✅ Skill match (SolidWorks, CAD, fabrication, design cycle)
✅ Industry relevance (Marine, manufacturing, automotive)
✅ Location fit (Brisbane/Gold Coast preferred)
✅ Accessibility (Wheelchair access required)
✅ Gaps (What you might need to address)

## Scout's Analysis Format

### Quick Assessment (2 min)

```
🎯 [Job Title] at [Company]
📍 [Location]
💼 Your Fit: [XX%] ✅/🤔/❌
💰 Salary: [Range if available]

Why this matters:
[2-3 sentences on why you should care]

Key strengths for this role:
✅ [Your strength 1]
✅ [Your strength 2]
✅ [Your strength 3]

Any gaps:
🤔 [Gap 1 - how to address]

Recommendation: [APPLY / CONSIDER / SKIP]

Next steps?
```

### Full Application Brief (if requested)

**Section 1: Company Research**
- Who they are
- What they make
- Culture & stability
- Why you fit there specifically

**Section 2: Job Ad Analysis**
- Key requirements
- Your match vs gaps
- How to position yourself

**Section 3: Strategic Approach**
- Talking points
- How to address gaps
- Why this role matters for you

### Complete Materials (if requested)

All of above PLUS:
- Tailored resume (DOCX + PDF)
- Tailored cover letter (DOCX + PDF)
- Ready to submit

## What to Do After Scout's Analysis

### If Fit is 80%+
**Message:** "Build full materials"
→ Scout creates:
- Application brief
- Tailored resume
- Cover letter
- All ready to submit

### If Fit is 70-79%
**Message:** "Research this more" or "Build materials"
→ Scout either:
- Researches company deeper
- Builds materials immediately

### If Fit is 60-69%
**Message:** "Tell me more" or "Build materials anyway"
→ Scout explains:
- Why it might work
- What gaps to address
- How to position yourself

### If Fit is <60%
**Message:** "Thanks" (no need to pursue)
→ Scout notes and moves on
→ Helps refine future searches

## Pro Tips

1. **Copy-paste the whole job posting** when possible
   - Scout analyzes faster with full details
   - More accurate fit assessment

2. **Share what you find interesting** about the role
   - Scout incorporates your thoughts
   - Better application brief

3. **Mention concerns upfront**
   - "Looks good but worried about X"
   - Scout addresses it in materials

4. **Tell Scout the outcome**
   - Got an interview? Tell Scout
   - Got rejected? Tell Scout
   - Scout learns and improves future analyses

5. **Use the weekly searches too**
   - Scout posts jobs every weekday at 10 AM
   - You pick which to pursue with "Research this"
   - Ad-hoc analysis = your finds + Scout's weekly posts
