## 2. SEO STRATEGY FOR DOMINATION

### 2.1 Local SEO (Gold Coast) – Quick Wins

**Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–4)**

1. **Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization**
   - Ensure NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) across all directories
   - Primary category: "Exercise Physiologist"
   - Secondary categories: "Physical Therapist," "Disability Services"
   - Add service areas: Gold Coast, Brisbane, Logan, Sunshine Coast
   - Upload 10+ professional photos (before/after, clinic, equipment)
   - Encourage 20+ Google reviews (target: 4.8+ stars)
   
   *Expected Impact:* 30% increase in GBP clicks within 4 weeks

2. **Citation Building** (Consistency + Authority)
   - Verify/update listings on:
     * Localsearch.com.au
     * Healthshare.com.au
     * NDIS Provider Finder
     * MyAgedCare (if applicable)
     * Industry directories (Allied Health Professionals Australia)
   - Ensure 100% NAP consistency across all platforms
   
   *Expected Impact:* Domain authority lift; Google trust signals

3. **Schema Markup Implementation**
   - Add LocalBusiness schema (address, phone, hours, service area)
   - Add FAQPage schema (common Q&A)
   - Add Review schema (structured rating data)
   - Add Service schema (condition-specific offerings)
   
   *Expected Impact:* Rich snippets in search results; click-through rate +15%

**Phase 2: Content Niche Focus (Weeks 5–12)**

Deploy the "Restart Exercise Physiology" model: **Injury-specific landing pages + hub-and-spoke blog linking**

**Target Conditions** (Priority Order):
1. Spinal cord injury (SCI) – all levels
2. Stroke rehabilitation
3. Traumatic brain injury (TBI)
4. Cerebral palsy (CP)
5. Multiple sclerosis (MS)
6. Amputation + prosthetics
7. Parkinson's disease
8. Post-polio syndrome

**Per-Condition Page Structure** (1,500–2,000 words):
- Condition overview + statistics
- How SCI/disability affects the body
- Your specialized approach (with credentials)
- Service offerings specific to that condition
- Testimonials (with consent) or case study snippets
- FAQ section (10–15 questions)
- Clear CTA: "Book a consultation"
- Transparent pricing (if applicable)

**Supporting Blog Content:**
- "3 exercises for SCI mobility" → links to SCI landing page
- "Returning to work after stroke: 6-week progression" → links to stroke page
- "NDIS funding for exercise physiology" → links to NDIS info
- "Why early intervention matters in TBI recovery" → links to TBI page

*Timeline:* 2 pages/week × 4 weeks = 8 pages by week 8

*Expected Impact:* 443 → 2,000+ tracked keywords (4–5 month horizon)

## 4. SOCIAL MEDIA & CONTENT MARKETING STRATEGY

|----------|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Facebook** | Community building, testimonial showcase | 3x/week | High (direct referrals) |

| **TikTok** | Short-form education, trending rehab tips | 2x/week | Medium (younger audience) |

### 4.2 Content Pillars (3-Month Rotation)

**Week 1: Education (30% of posts)**
- "5 myths about SCI exercise physiology"
- "How NDIS funding covers exercise therapy"
- "Exercise progression for post-stroke recovery"

**Week 2: Social Proof (30% of posts)**
- Client testimonials (video preferred)
- Before/after outcomes (anonymized)
- Success stories ("3 months post-referral")
- Team credentials/certifications

**Week 3: Engagement (25% of posts)**
- Q&A responses ("Your questions about SCI rehab")
- Behind-the-scenes (clinic setup, equipment)
- "Did you know?" facts about disability rehab
- Interactive polls/quizzes

**Week 4: Offers/CTAs (15% of posts)**
- Free consultation promotions
- "Book this week" incentives
- NDIS planning guides (lead magnet)
- Webinar invitations

### 4.3 Video Content (High-Impact)

**Tier 1: Case Studies** (3–5 min, quarterly)
- Interview format (practitioner + client, with consent)
- Condition + outcome journey
- Funding mechanism explained
- Distribution: YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, website

**Tier 2: Education Series** (2–3 min, bi-weekly)
- "Monday motivation" exercise tips
- Condition-specific education
- NDIS process walkthroughs
- Accessibility features (captions always)

**Tier 3: Short-Form** (15–30 sec, 2x/week)
- TikTok/Instagram Reels: quick tips, exercise snippets
- #DisabilityCommunity trending topics

**Production Strategy:**
- Batch record quarterly (1 day of filming = 12 videos)
- Cost: $200–500/video (local freelancer)
- ROI: Estimated 5–10% conversion on viewers

### 4.4 Review Generation (Critical for Local Ranking)

**Strategy:**
1. Ask every client at session 4 & 10
2. Email + SMS follow-up with review link
3. Incentive: Monthly prize draw (free session)
4. Target: 20+ new reviews/month

**Response Time:** Aim for 24–48 hour replies to all reviews

*Expected Impact:* 4.8+ star average within 3 months; 30% increase in click-through
