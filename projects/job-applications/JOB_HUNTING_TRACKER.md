### Tracking Fields
- **Company:** Company name
- **Role:** Job title
- **Posted:** Date posted
- **Source:** Where found (LinkedIn, Seek, Indeed, etc.)
- **Match %:** How well Paul's background fits (0-100%)
- **Status:** Found / Researching / Brief Ready / Materials Ready / Submitted / Interview / Rejected / Accepted
- **Notes:** Key context, red flags, opportunities
- **Applied Date:** When submitted
- **Follow-up Date:** When to follow up if no response
- **Outcome:** Result (interview, rejection, offer, etc.)

## Fit Assessment Criteria

**60%+ = Good Fit**
- Core role matches Paul's background
- 60-70% of requirements met
- Clear value proposition

**70%+ = Very Good Fit**
- Strong experience match
- 70-85% of requirements met
- Excited about the role

**80%+ = Excellent Fit**
- Excellent experience match
- 85%+ of requirements met
- This is the kind of role Paul should be doing

**<60% = Skip**
- Too many gaps
- Role doesn't match
- Unlikely to succeed

## Success Metrics

- **Application quality:** Every submission is polished and personalized
- **Fit accuracy:** Only apply to roles with 60%+ match
- **Response rate:** Track % of applications that get responses
- **Interview conversion:** % of applications → interviews
- **Offer success:** Goal is genuine interest, not just volume
