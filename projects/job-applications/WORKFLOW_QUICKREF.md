# Job Hunt Workflow — Quick Reference

## The Process (4 Steps)

### Step 1️⃣ — Job Discovery (Scout Automatic)
- Scout searches LinkedIn, Seek, Indeed
- Posts top opportunities here with fit scores
- Your move: Say "research" or "skip"

### Step 2️⃣ — Company Research (You Approve)
- Scout researches company: who they are, what they do, culture, stability
- Creates research brief with key context
- Your move: Review brief, say "build materials" or "pass"

### Step 3️⃣ — Build Resume & Cover Letter (Scout Automatic)
- Scout tailors resume from your templates
- Customizes for this specific role
- Writes cover letter with company research insights
- Generates DOCX + PDF versions
- Your move: Review, request changes, say "ready to submit"

### Step 4️⃣ — Submit & Track (You Manual + Scout Tracking)
- You submit resume + cover letter
- Scout logs application in tracker
- Follow up reminders in tracker
- Your move: Tell Scout outcome (interviewed, rejected, etc.)

## Fit Scoring System

Scout only presents jobs with 60%+ fit to your background.

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 80%+ | Excellent fit | **Apply** — This is a great match |
| 70-79% | Very good fit | **Consider** — Strong candidate |
| 60-69% | Good fit | **Explore** — Worth pursuing |
| <60% | Skip | Don't waste time |

## Red Flags Scout Watches For

✅ **Green flags:** Company investing in engineering, new projects, growth
⚠️ **Caution:** Company in transition, unclear stability
❌ **Red flag:** Multi-story building with no elevator, manual labour focus, poor disability accessibility
❌ **Skip:** Company with culture issues, unrealistic expectations, wages too low

## Dates & Follow-Ups

Scout will track:
- **Posted date** — When job was posted (first 2 weeks are hot)
- **Applied date** — When you submitted
- **Follow-up date** — When to follow up if no response (7-10 days typical)
- **Outcome date** — When you hear back (interview, rejection, etc.)

Scout will remind you to follow up if:
- 10+ days pass with no response
- Interview is scheduled
- You've told Scout you haven't heard back

## Tips for Success

1. **Respond fast:** When Scout posts a job you like, say "research" same day
2. **Review carefully:** Read Scout's research before saying "build materials"
3. **Update Scout:** Tell Scout what you think about roles/companies (helps refine future searches)
4. **Submit promptly:** Once materials are ready, apply within 1-2 days (jobs move fast)
5. **Follow up:** Scout will remind you, but mark calendar if you prefer (7-10 days typical)
6. **Track outcomes:** Tell Scout if you got interview, rejection, etc. (helps everyone learn)

## Accessibility & Disability Strategy

Scout respects your privacy. Disability is mentioned strategically:
- ✅ Mention if it strengthens story (e.g., "I design with accessibility in mind")
- ❌ Don't mention if it's not relevant
- ✅ Always verify wheelchair access early (Scout screens for this)
- ✅ Be honest about fatigue limits in interviews (sets realistic expectations)

Remember: Your disability is part of who you are, but it's not your whole story. Scout helps you present the full picture.
