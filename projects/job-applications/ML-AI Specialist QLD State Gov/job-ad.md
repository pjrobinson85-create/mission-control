## Position Overview
The ML/AI Specialist will design, develop, and deliver machine learning and artificial intelligence solutions and provide strategic guidance on policy and frameworks as part of an enterprise GenAI Adoption Program. 

The role will progress Statements of Need through rapid Proofs of Concept (PoCs), pilots, and preparation for production—ensuring compliance with government enterprise architecture, information security, privacy, and responsible AI principles while providing operational readiness insights for handover to BAU/operations.

The position contributes to program governance by producing clear technical evidence to support adoption decisions.
