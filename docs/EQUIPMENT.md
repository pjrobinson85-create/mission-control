**Status:** Research in progress

**What it is:** A powered attachment system (likely for wheelchair integration).

**Specs needed for bike rack design:**

## Bike Rack Design Goal

Design a car-mounted bike rack capable of safely holding either the jeetrike or the batec scrambler (used interchangeably).

**Constraints:**
- Must support both vehicles
- Must be securable and safe while driving
- Must be practical for frequent use
- Must consider weight distribution on vehicle
