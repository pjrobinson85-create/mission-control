================================================================================
                         HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS
================================================================================

STEP 1: JOB DISCOVERY
- Scout searches LinkedIn, Seek, Indeed
- Filters for mechanical engineer roles in Queensland
- Scores each opportunity (0-100%)
- Only presents 60%+ fits
- Posts opportunity in Telegram with summary

STEP 2: YOUR RESPONSE
Paul responds in Telegram:
- "Research this" → Scout investigates company
- "Skip" → Scout moves to next opportunity
- Silence → Scout follows up next day

STEP 3: COMPANY RESEARCH
- Scout researches company (who, what, size, culture, stability)
- Creates detailed application brief (3 sections)
- Posts brief in Telegram: "Research complete. Build materials?"

STEP 4: YOUR APPROVAL
Paul responds:
- "Build materials" → Scout creates resume + cover letter
- "Pass" → Scout moves to next opportunity
- "Tell me more" → Scout provides additional info

STEP 5: APPLICATION MATERIALS
- Scout selects best resume template for role
- Customizes resume for this specific opportunity
- Generates cover letter from company research
- Creates DOCX and PDF versions
- Posts to Telegram: "Ready to review?"

STEP 6: YOUR REVIEW
Paul reviews materials and responds:
- "Ready to submit" → Paul submits application
- "Update [section]" → Scout revises and resends
- "Let me think" → Scout waits

STEP 7: SUBMISSION & TRACKING
- Paul submits application
- Scout logs in JOB_HUNTING_TRACKER.md
- Scout reminds Paul to follow up after 7-10 days if needed
- Paul reports outcome (interview, rejection, etc.)
- Scout logs result for learning
