## How to Use Scout

### When Scout Posts a Job
Scout message in Telegram:
```
🎯 [Job Title] at [Company]
Your Fit: [XX%] ✅
Why: [Explanation]
Research? [Y/N]
```

Your response:
- "Research this" → Scout investigates
- "Skip" → Scout moves on
- Silence → Scout follows up

### When Scout Posts Research Brief
Scout provides detailed company analysis (3 sections):
- Section 1: Company Research (who, what, culture, stability)
- Section 2: Job Analysis (requirements vs your fit)
- Section 3: Strategic Approach (talking points, strategy)

Your response:
- "Build materials" → Scout creates resume + cover letter
- "Pass" → Scout moves on
- "Tell me more" → Scout provides additional info

### When Scout Provides Materials
Scout generates and posts:
- Tailored resume (DOCX + PDF)
- Tailored cover letter (DOCX + PDF)
- Ready for your review

Your response:
- "Ready to submit" → You submit application
- "Update [section]" → Scout revises
- "Let me think" → Scout waits

### Anytime You Want
Text Scout:
- "Find [industry] jobs"
- "Research [company]"
- "How does [job] fit?"
- "When should I follow up?"
